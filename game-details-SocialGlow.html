Social Glow

A social life sim, and an artistic commentary on Cancel Culture.

Gameplay Trailer

Game Overview & Development Insights

The Vision: Have the player think critically about the nature of cancel culture in society.

Social Glow stemmed from the question, why is cancel culture so scary? The answer, because it sneaks up on us.

The game uses a dual status system to track the player's social and professional status, and without showing them the changes, inches the player closer to, or further away from victory. This aspect of 'going in blind' echos the feeling of real society, where you dont always know if you can speak your mind, becauseyou could easily find yourself on the bad side of social media without ever realizing it.

Key Game Features

Dual Status System

Tracks and changes your socail and professional status simultaneously and you move through the game dialog.

Mandatory Side Tasks

Each environment gives the player 3 side tasks, of which 2 must be completed before they are allowed to progress with the story. This prevents repetitive stale gameplay, keeping the player enguaged for longer.

Technical Specifications

  • Engine: Unity
  • Primary Libraries: Unity library suit
  • Duration: 1 week (Group Development)
  • Current Status: Public Beta 0.3
  • Target Platform: Mobile

Challenges Solved

Used satirical examples to make commentary on cancel culture without allowing the game to become too political. Used side tasks to increase the complexity of the game, making it more enguaging that a simple point and click system.
